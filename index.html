Our Story

Welcome to Schoolhouse Grounds, where every cup tells a story. Nestled in our remote corner of the world, we've been crafting the perfect coffee experience since our doors first opened.

We source the finest seasonal ingredients and roast our beans to perfection, creating signature blends that warm the soul. Our family recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring every pastry and sandwich carries the taste of tradition.

More than just a coffee shop, we're a gathering place for our community – where friends meet, ideas brew, and memories are made over steaming cups of our signature cold brew and freshly baked treats.
